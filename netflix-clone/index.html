Pokémon: Detective Pikachu

Detective Harry Goodman's disappearance prompts his son Tim to search for him. By his side he has Pikachu, his father's old Pokémon partner, who has recently lost his memory. Together, they roam the streets of the metropolis of Ryme City, where humans and Pokémon live in harmony... for now.
